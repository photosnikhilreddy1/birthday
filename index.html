WindMill: 20 June 2025

A windmill turning slow.
Our first beer.
Your eyes speaking quietly to mine.

The bike ride after—cool air, soft silence, shared space.

Your hand on my shoulder—warm, gentle, grounding.
A touch that stayed in my mind long after.

And somewhere inside, a small wish began—
a simple side hug, someday.

A tiny beginning. A quiet start. But it was the start of us.

If there’s one truth my heart knows now,
it’s that you’re the warmth I return to,
the chaos I crave,
the softness I fall into,
and the story I want to keep writing —
again, again, and always.
